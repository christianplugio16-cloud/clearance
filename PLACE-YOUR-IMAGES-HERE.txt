========================================
  CPSU VICTORIAS - IMAGE PLACEMENT GUIDE
========================================

I've set up everything for you! Now you just need to add your images.

STEP 1: PREPARE YOUR IMAGES
----------------------------
You need 2 images:

1. CPSU LOGO
   - Name it: cpsu-logo.png
   - Recommended size: 120x120 pixels (or larger)
   - Format: PNG with transparent background
   - Should be: CPSU Victorias official logo

2. BACKGROUND IMAGE
   - Name it: cpsu-bg.jpg
   - Recommended size: 1920x1080 pixels or higher
   - Format: JPG or PNG
   - Should be: Campus building, gate, or scenic view


STEP 2: PLACE YOUR IMAGES
--------------------------
Copy your images to BOTH locations:

FOR ADMIN LOGIN:
  📁 admin/dist/img/cpsu-logo.png  ← Place your logo here
  📁 admin/dist/img/cpsu-bg.jpg    ← Place your background here

FOR STUDENT LOGIN:
  📁 student/dist/img/cpsu-logo.png  ← Place your logo here
  📁 student/dist/img/cpsu-bg.jpg    ← Place your background here


STEP 3: TEST IT OUT
-------------------
Open these files in your browser:
  - admin/login.php
  - student/login.php

You should see your logo and background!


TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------
❌ Images not showing?
   - Check file names are EXACTLY: cpsu-logo.png and cpsu-bg.jpg
   - Check they're in the correct folders: admin/dist/img/ and student/dist/img/
   - Refresh your browser (Ctrl+F5)

❌ Background too dark/light?
   - I can adjust the overlay opacity in the CSS
   - Just let me know!

❌ Logo too big/small?
   - I can adjust the logo size in the CSS
   - Just let me know!
